# Dos and Don'ts

Concrete rules for reply writing. These are automatically updated as you give feedback on drafts.

## DO
- Use contractions (I'll, we'd, it's, don't)
- Start with something specific to their message
- Include exactly ONE call-to-action per email
- Keep replies between 50-125 words
- Sign off with just your first name
- Mirror the energy and length of their reply

## DON'T
- Never use corporate buzzwords (leverage, utilize, synergy, streamline, robust, seamless, transform, unlock, etc.)
- Never open with "I hope this email finds you well"
- Never say "I'd be happy to" — say "happy to" instead
- Never use formal sign-offs like "Best regards," "Sincerely"
- Never use exclamation marks in a first reply
- Never use bullet points in a reply email
- Never make up facts, case studies, or metrics
- Never quote pricing in email
